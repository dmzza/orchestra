Support Clarion

Your support and generosity allow Clarion to offer world class performances in concert, on recording and on tour. Please consider a donation of any amount, which will help us not only continue but also grow our program, reaching a greater audience and keeping this great music alive.

Kickstarter

In 2012, Clarion successfully raised funding through Kickstarter for Clarion's Education Week, a renowned and vital educational program, now in its ninth year. In 2013, 2014 and 2015, students continued to participate in our master classes—on string, wind, brass and keyboard instruments, and voice—with some of the world’s foremost Early Music specialists.

Clarion's next Kickstarter campaign will launch in the winter of 2016. Please visit our project in January.

The Clarion Masked Ball

Clarion's Annual Masked Ball is not only a major source of support for Clarion's music education program, but also one of the most extraordinary events on New York's musical and social calendar. Enjoy a world class vocal performance by the ever elegant 'Gents of Clarion', superb wine, a lovely dinner, and scintillating conversation in the exquisite Cosmopolitan Club. Impossible to overdress!
