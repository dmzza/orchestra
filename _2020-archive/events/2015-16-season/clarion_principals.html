A Viennese Reverie with Principals of The Clarion Orchestra

F.J. Haydn - String Quartet No. 62, Op. 76 No. 3 ‘Emperor’
W.A. Mozart – String Quartet No. 21 in D Major, K. 575

Cynthia Roberts and Theresa Salomon, violins
Daniel Elyar, viola
Myron Lutzke, cello

Four of Clarion’s virtuoso principal players join forces to present Haydn’s beloved ‘Emperor’ string quartet and the first of Mozart’s ‘Prussian’ string quartets, written for Friedrich Wilhelm II of Prussia, an avid cellist. Internationally renowned individually as masters of early music, our players recently performed chamber music together in the Clarion Orchestral Series and in a private concert at the Frick Collection.
