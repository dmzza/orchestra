PRINCIPALS OF THE CLARION ORCHESTRA
1.15pm >
22 October 2015 – St. Bartholomew’s Church, 325 Park (50th Street)

Internationally renowned individually as masters of early music, four of Clarion’s virtuoso principal players join forces to perform Haydn’s beloved ‘Emperor’ string quartet and the first of Mozart’s ‘Prussian’ string quartets.

F.J. Haydn - String Quartet No. 62, Op. 76 No. 3 ‘Emperor’
W.A. Mozart – String Quartet No. 21 in D Major, K. 575

Cynthia Roberts and Theresa Salomon, violins
Daniel Elyar, viola
Myron Lutzke, cello

Free and open to the public.

RACHMANINOFF AT TRINITY WALL STREET -- 2 performances
5pm, 31 December 2015 & 8pm, 3 January 2016 – St. Paul's Chapel , 209 Broadway (Fulton/Vesey)

After last season’s sold-out and widely acclaimed performances of Rachmaninoff’s Vespers in Trinity Wall Street’s Twelfth Night Festival, the Clarion Choir returns with Rachmaninoff’s towering and incandescent Liturgy of St. John Chrysostom to mark the close of 2015 and welcome 2016. Join us for this rarely-heard masterpiece.

Free and open to the public.
