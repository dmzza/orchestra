clarion masked gala

The annual Masked Gala is not only an important fundraiser for Clarion's programs, but also has become an important event on New York’s social calendar! This year Clarion honors Barbaralee Diamonstein-Spielvogel & Ambassador Carl Spielvogel who have provided essential leadership in the Arts over many years in New York City as well as on the State and National levels. Please join us for an evening of extraordinary performances by Clarion musicians, cocktails and a three-course dinner at the elegant Metropolitan Club overlooking Central Park.

Journal Ads

A souvenir journal from the Gala will feature congratulations and greetings from friends and well-wishers, along with photos and briefs on Barbaralee and Carl’s accomplishments. Information on sponsorships and advertising is listed below. Please contact Lauren Ishida at lauren@clarionsociety.org with any questions or for more information.
