Bel Canto...on Period Instruments

The period-instrument Clarion Orchestra, together with two rising opera stars –  tenor Mario Chang, currently singing the role of Nemorino in L'elisir d'amore at the Metropolitan Opera, and soprano Hyesang Park of the Metropolitan Opera’s Lindemann Young Artist Program and a 2015 prize winner of the Operalia competition – explore the sound world of Rossini and Donizetti, performing bel canto arias and duets as well as orchestral music from their operas Il turco in Italia, Lucia di Lammermoor, Le duc d'Albe, and L'elisir d'amore.

Performers:

Hyesang Park, soprano
Mario Chang, tenor
The Clarion Orchestra
Steven Fox, conductor
