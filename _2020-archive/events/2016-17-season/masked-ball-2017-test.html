CLARION 60TH ANNIVERSARY MASKED GALA

Honoring: Tatiana Pouschine

Join the Clarion Music Society on April 4, 2017 as we celebrate the 60th anniversary of our founding with a Masked Gala at the elegant and storied JW Marriott Essex House. We have many accomplishments to celebrate over the last six decades, and our honoree and dear friend Tatiana Pouschine has done much to ensure that Clarion’s present and future are equally sparkling.

The gala begins with a lively cocktail hour. Enjoy our signature libation and delectable passed hors d’oeuvres while listening to live music by Clarion musicians as you peruse the unique offerings of our silent auction. We will then move into the ballroom for a seated dinner and a journey through Clarion's history, as well as more glorious music performed by orchestra members and some special guests.

Black tie. Bring your own mask, or wear one of ours.
