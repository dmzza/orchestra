CD release party at shapiro gallery

Join us to celebrate The Clarion Choir's recording release of Passion Week by Maximilian Steinberg, which was launched earlier this month on the Naxos label and already has been receiving enthusiastic responses from critics and CD collectors!

 

Join us for wine and hors d'oeuvres at
Shapiro Auction Gallery
506 East 74th Street (east of York)
Wednesday, 14 September, 6.30-8.30

 

There is no cover charge for this event.

However, the CD will be available for purchase, and proceeds will support Clarion's upcoming premiere of Passion Week in Moscow, St. Petersburg and London.
