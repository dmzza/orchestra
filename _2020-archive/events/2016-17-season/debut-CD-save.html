Passion Week Debut Recording, August, 2016

We are thrilled to announce the first CD recording of The Clarion Choir under the direction of Steven Fox, to be released this August on the Naxos label.

Maximilien Steinberg's PASSION WEEK was recorded in October and November of 2014, following Clarion's 'stunning' (The New York Times) premiere. The review raved, 'The work is a treasure. Steinberg's style, with its contrapuntal complexities and its enriched harmonies, is slightly advanced over that of Rachmaninoff.' The Clarion Choir's interpetation also includes several ancient Russian chants upon which some of the movements were based.

More details regarding how to get your copy of this exciting new CD will be posted here before August 1.
