clarion masked gala

The annual Masked Gala is not only an important fundraiser for Clarion's programs, but also has become an important event on New York’s social calendar!

This year Clarion honors Christine Stonbely, Clarion’s Board President (2008-present). Please join us for an evening of extraordinary performances by Clarion musicians, cocktails and a three-course dinner at the elegant Metropolitan Club overlooking Central Park.
