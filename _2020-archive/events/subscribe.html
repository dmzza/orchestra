Clarion's 2019-20 Subscription Series

We invite you to subscribe to our 2019-2020 season today! Subscribe now to ensure that you won't miss out on any of this year's world-class performances.

Join the GRAMMY-nominated Clarion Choir, The Clarion Orchestra and Steven Fox for a concert of motets by Bach and Brahms on September 26, and again as we ring in the New Year with Rachmaninoff's All-Night Vigil ('Vespers'). On February 13, The Orchestra Modern presents Nikolai Medtner's Piano Concerto No. 2 and Tchaikovsky's riveting final symphony, No. 6, the "Pathetique" featuring Russian piano virtuoso, Ilya Maximov. Finally, on May 5, The Clarion Choir and The Clarion Orchestra join forces to perform to Bach's seminal masterpiece, the Mass in B Minor.

Subscribers at the General Admission and Orchestra levels receive 10% off the price of admission. Orchestra Prime subscribers receive 15% off. Save the dates, and book your subscription today!
