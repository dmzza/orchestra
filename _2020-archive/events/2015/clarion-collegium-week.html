Clarion Collegium Week

High school and college students, and young professional musicians work with principals of The Clarion Orchestra in music of the Viennese Classic period, with a special focus on the chamber music of Beethoven on original instruments.

Saturday, May 30th 2015:
1pm to 2.30pm - Baroque cello with Clarion principal cello Myron Lutzke
4pm to 6pm - Vocal with Clarion soloist Rufus Müller, tenor

Saturday, June 6th 2015:
5pm to 6.30pm - Baroque flute with Clarion principal flute Sandra Miller
7.30pm to 9pm - Baroque violin and viola with Clarion concertmaster Cynthia Roberts

To enquire about participating in classes, please contact Brian Ford at bford@clarionsociety.org.
