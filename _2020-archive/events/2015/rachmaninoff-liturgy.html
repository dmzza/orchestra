RACHMANINOFF'S LITURGY OF ST. JOHN CHRYSOSTOM

After last season’s sold-out and widely acclaimed performances of Rachmaninoff’s Vespers in Trinity Wall Street’s Twelfth Night Festival, the Clarion Choir returns with Rachmaninoff’s towering and incandescent Liturgy of St. John Chrysostom to mark the close of 2015 and welcome 2016. Join us for this rarely-heard masterpiece.
